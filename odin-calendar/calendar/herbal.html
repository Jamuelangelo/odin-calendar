The Herbal Calendar

The Herbal Calendar has 10 months of 5 weeks. Each month is represented by a tree. Each week is represented by a branch.There is also a rotation of the 5 elements. There are 2 spare weeks at the end of the 10 months

  • Birch
  • Oak
  • Maple
  • Pine
  • Spruce
  • Aspen
  • Alder
  • Rowan
  • Juniper
  • Chestnut
