The Primal Calendar

\

The Primal Calendar has 13 months of 4 weeks. Each month is represented by an animal. Each week is associated with one of the 4 elements. There is a spare day at the end of the 13 months. (2 spare days on leap years)

  • Bear
  • Seal
  • Lynx
  • Owl
  • Moose
  • Wolf
  • Deer
  • Wolverine
  • Crow
  • Fox
  • Squirrel
  • Beaver
  • Hedgehog
